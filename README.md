# SparkTS-Localized-Innovation-Control-in-MUSIC-Generation

Language model-based music generation faces a dilemma: play it safe, and the output is highly predictable; turn up the randomness, and the song collapses into chaos. However, the soul of any great track is based on those unpredictable highlights, which we call "sparks". Current methods frequently ruin the entire track when attempting to creating such a moment of innovation. Therefore, we introduce SparkTS, a tuning-free decoding framework based on constrained Monte Carlo Tree Search. Considering general metrics are insufficient for localized innovation evaluation, we also designed three new composite metrics (SCI, SLS, and SCS) for spark-specific analysis. The experiment results show that SparkTS successfully generates intense, perfectly timed musical sparks without sacrificing the overall quality and coherence.
